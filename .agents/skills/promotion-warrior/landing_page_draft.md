## 🚀 The Core Philosophy: "Hybrid Production"

In 2026, no single tool does it all perfectly. The secret to high-converting video is **combining the best engine for each specific task**.

### 1. The "Script & Logic" Layer
- **Tool**: Claude 3.7 / GPT-5
- **Goal**: Don't just ask for a script. Ask for a "Hook-Body-CTA" framework with specific visual cues for b-roll.

### 2. The "Visual Foundation" (B-Roll)
- **Tool**: Midjourney (Images) + Kling 3 / Runway Gen-3 (Motion)
- **Why**: Use these for environmental shots, abstract concepts, and cinematic hooks.
- **Pro Tip**: Use a consistent seed for images to maintain environment consistency.

### 3. The "Human Connection" (Talking Heads)
- **Tool**: **[HeyGen](https://www.heygen.com/?sid=rewardful&utm_content=creator&utm_medium=affiliate&via=samantha)**
- **Why**: We've tested 15+ avatar tools. HeyGen's **Avatar IV/V** models are the only ones with 100% natural lip-sync for scripts over 60 seconds.
- **Killer Feature**: **Video Translation**. If you're going global, their lip-sync translation is magic.

### 4. The "Assembly & Polish"
- **Tool**: CapCut (Social) / DaVinci Resolve (Professional)
- **Goal**: Stitching it all together. Add captions, trending audio, and sound effects (SFX) to hide the "AI feel".

## 💡 Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- ❌ **Over-reliance on one avatar**: Keep the script punchy and cut to b-roll every 5-7 seconds.
- ❌ **Robot voices**: Always use a high-quality voice model (ElevenLabs or HeyGen's premium voices).
- ❌ **Ignoring the Hook**: The first 3 seconds must be real or extremely high-quality cinematic AI.
