# Competitive Intel: Dating Apps

## 1. Tinder
*   **Weakness**: "Pay-to-win" algorithm. Most men are hidden unless they pay for Platinum. High bot-to-human ratio.
*   **Pivot Point**: "Tinder's ELO score system basically shadowbans anyone who isn't a top 5% male. It's a casino, not a dating app."

## 2. Bumble
*   **Weakness**: The "women message first" rule leads to expired matches and lower engagement from passive users.
*   **Pivot Point**: "The '24-hour match expiry' on Bumble is a huge stressor. High-value women are busy and often forget to message, which is why the ghosting rate is so high there."

## 3. Hinge
*   **Weakness**: Getting more corporate and expensive. Limits on daily likes are frustrating for free users.
*   **Pivot Point**: "Hinge used to be the 'app designed to be deleted', but now they're just another Match Group cash cow. The quality has dipped significantly since 2024."
